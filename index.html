About Rolland Ashiteye

Hi there! I am Rolland Ashiteye from Accra, Ghana. I currently work as a graduate intern at the West African Centre for Cell Biology of Infectious Pathogens (WACCBIP) where I write algorithms for analyzing Genomic Data using python . I have over two years experience creating software and websites for both clients and myself. I also have several years of experience assisting people in troubleshooting issues they face when using their computers. I have experience programming in many different languages such as C++, C#, Java, Python, PHP and Javascript.

I graduated recently from the University of Cape Coast (UCC), Cape Coast having studied B.Sc. Computer Science. I have also completed several online courses to polish most of the skills I learnt in school. I enjoy learning everyday and also applying what I learn to solve problems around me.
